# **Catalyzing \*omics innovation**

*What:* hackseq is a open-science genomics hackathon. It's a great place to meet like-minded individuals, learn, and create!

*Who:* hackseq is open to everyone with coding experience. The focus is on empowering students and trainees who want to apply their skills and knowledge in a creative new way.

*When:* hackseq18 happened October 12-14 2018.

*Where:* UBC Life Sciences Center -- Vancouver, BC. Canada

\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*\*

**100% DIY:** Share an idea. Form a team. Hack a solution together.

## The Projects
hackseq18 was bigger then ever. We had 80 participants join together to tackle 8 challenging bioinformatics problems. [Learn more](https://hackseq.github.io/hs18/2018/08/11/Projects2018.txt.html) about each of the projects.
